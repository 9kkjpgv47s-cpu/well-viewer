Get all 414,953 wells on the live site
======================================

1. Put all 8 chunk files in the project folder.

   If you moved chunks 1–7 to Desktop earlier, run:

   cd "/Users/dominiceasterling/DNR_Well_Viewer_Full_Demo"
   mv ~/Desktop/dnr_wells_chunk_1.csv.gz ~/Desktop/dnr_wells_chunk_2.csv.gz ~/Desktop/dnr_wells_chunk_3.csv.gz ~/Desktop/dnr_wells_chunk_4.csv.gz ~/Desktop/dnr_wells_chunk_5.csv.gz ~/Desktop/dnr_wells_chunk_6.csv.gz ~/Desktop/dnr_wells_chunk_7.csv.gz .

   You should have all of these in the folder:
   dnr_wells_chunk_0.csv.gz through dnr_wells_chunk_7.csv.gz

2. Deploy.

   npx vercel --prod

3. Open your site (e.g. https://c-j-well-viewer.vercel.app). The app will load chunk 0, then 1, then 2 … through 7 and show “Loaded XXXXX wells” when done. That number should be 414,953.

Done. The site now has all the wells.
