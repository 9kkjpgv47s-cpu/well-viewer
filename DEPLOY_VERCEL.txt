DEPLOY WELL VIEWER TO VERCEL (new project)
=========================================

Run in Terminal:

  cd "/Users/dominiceasterling/DNR_Well_Viewer_Full_Demo"
  npx vercel

When Vercel asks:

  "Link to existing project?"
  → Type  N  and press Enter   (do NOT choose "yes" or the claude-suite project)

  "What's your project's name?"
  → Type  dnr-well-viewer  (or any name) and press Enter

Vercel will create a NEW project and give you a URL like:
  https://dnr-well-viewer-xxxx.vercel.app

Open that URL in Safari on your phone — the map will work.

If you choose "Link to existing project? yes" and pick 9kkjpgv47s-cpu-claude-suite-mobile,
you will get the ".next" error. Always choose NO so this folder gets its own project.
